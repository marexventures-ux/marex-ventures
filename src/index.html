Frequently Asked Questions

Is my company data secure when using your custom software?

Absolutely. For custom solutions, we utilize strictly private deployments or enterprise APIs that do not train on your company's proprietary data. Security and privacy are integrated into the architecture from day one.

Does my team need to know how to code?

Not at all! Our systems are entirely "Done-For-You." We build intuitive front-end interfaces or integrate directly into the tools your team already uses (like Slack, Google Workspace, or your existing CRM). We also provide comprehensive training during handover.

How long does a custom system integration take?

It varies depending on the complexity of your workflows. However, most MVP (Minimum Viable Product) automations are developed, tested, and deployed within 2 to 4 weeks after the initial Discovery phase.
