The Story

Starting Marex Ventures in 2019 as a digital marketing business, Mary encountered the same challenges any growing business faces—inefficient workflows, manual processes, and high costs for custom systems.

The turning point: When a client needed an attendance tracking system quoted far beyond their budget, Mary bypassed traditional development outsourcing and built a custom automated solution herself. That single system saved the client roughly 1.7 Million Naira.

This reshaped her perspective entirely. She realized businesses face these hurdles daily but lack affordable, practical systems. Thus, Marex Ventures evolved into a custom solutions company focused on scalable software and automation.

Signature Positioning

Mary Mmoh is a Nigeria-based founder who builds, deploys, and ships production-level custom software and SaaS products on enterprise-grade infrastructure — without a formal computer science background.

She designs and deploys functional software systems with real users, authentication, and workflows. Her work challenges the assumption that building AI systems is strictly limited to traditionally trained software engineers.
